Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Space Decor Belle collects, uses, and protects the information you share with us when you visit our website, contact our team, request a consultation, or use our services.

2. Information We May Collect

We may collect basic details that help us understand your enquiry and provide better service, including:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address.
  • Your project type, property details, location, and design requirements.
  • Messages or information submitted through forms, WhatsApp, phone calls, or email.
  • Basic website usage data such as pages visited and interaction behaviour.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only for professional and service-related purposes, such as:

  • Responding to your enquiry or consultation request.
  • Understanding your interior, Vastu, decor, or design needs.
  • Sharing project updates, service details, quotations, or follow-up communication.
  • Improving our website, services, client experience, and communication process.

4. Data Protection

We take reasonable steps to keep your personal information safe. Your details are handled carefully and are not sold, rented, or misused. Access to your information is limited to authorised team members who need it for service communication.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal data. In some cases, we may share limited information with trusted team members, vendors, consultants, or service partners only when required to support your project or respond to your request.

6. Cookies & Website Analytics

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics tools to understand visitor activity and improve the website experience. These tools help us know which pages are useful and how users interact with our content.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may include links to third-party platforms such as WhatsApp, social media, maps, or external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those third-party platforms.

8. Your Rights

You may contact us to request correction, update, or removal of your personal information from our communication records, subject to reasonable business or legal requirements.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our website means you accept the updated policy.
